Diet & Fitness Tips

Underweight

To gain healthy weight, try these tips:

  • Eat calorie-dense foods like nuts, avocados, and dairy.
  • Increase protein intake (chicken, eggs, beans) to build muscle.

Normal

Maintain a balanced lifestyle:

  • Continue eating a balanced diet (vegetables, lean protein, whole grains).
  • Stay active with at least 30 minutes of daily exercise.
  • Stay hydrated and manage stress through mindfulness or yoga.
  • Monitor your weight regularly to maintain a healthy BMI.

Overweight

To achieve a healthier weight:

  • Reduce sugary foods and processed snacks.
  • Eat high-fiber foods (vegetables, whole grains) to stay full longer.
  • Exercise regularly: walking, jogging, or gym workouts.
  • Drink plenty of water and avoid sugary drinks.

Obese

For better health, follow these steps:

  • Follow a calorie-controlled, nutrient-dense diet.
  • Increase physical activity (start with 20-30 min daily walks).
  • Avoid fast food, fried foods, and sugary beverages.
  • Consult a doctor or nutritionist for a personalized diet plan.
